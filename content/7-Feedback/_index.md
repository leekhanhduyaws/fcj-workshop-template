# Feedback and Suggestions

The following reflects my personal experience throughout the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** internship program. These comments are based on my own observations and are intended to provide constructive feedback that may help improve the program for future participants.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The working environment at FCAJ is highly professional, welcoming, and collaborative. Throughout the internship, every team member was approachable and willing to provide assistance whenever I encountered challenges, even outside regular working hours. The positive atmosphere encouraged open communication and made it easy to exchange ideas, ask questions, and learn from others. In addition, I believe the program could be further enhanced by organizing more team-building activities or informal networking sessions to strengthen relationships among members.

### 2. Support from Mentors and Team Administrators

One of the most valuable aspects of the internship was the continuous guidance provided by the mentors. They explained technical concepts clearly, patiently answered questions, and shared practical industry experience that extended beyond theoretical knowledge. What impressed me the most was their mentoring approach—they encouraged interns to analyze problems independently and explore possible solutions before offering guidance. This approach significantly improved my critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Furthermore, the Team Admin members were always responsive and supportive, ensuring that documentation, schedules, and administrative tasks were handled efficiently throughout the internship.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

As a **Software Engineering** student, I found the assigned tasks highly relevant to my academic background. During the internship, I had the opportunity to apply knowledge related to software development, database management, system design, and software engineering practices while simultaneously exploring cloud technologies through AWS services. This combination of software engineering principles and cloud computing provided me with a broader perspective on how modern applications are designed, deployed, and maintained in production environments. The experience effectively bridged the gap between academic learning and real-world software development.

### 4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development

The internship provided numerous opportunities to acquire both technical and professional skills. In addition to learning about AWS services and cloud architecture, I improved my ability to read technical documentation, analyze system requirements, prepare technical reports, manage project tasks, and follow a structured software development workflow. I also strengthened my communication, teamwork, and presentation skills through regular collaboration with mentors and fellow interns. These experiences have increased my confidence and provided valuable preparation for my future career in software engineering and cloud technologies.

### 5. Company Culture and Teamwork

One of the most memorable aspects of FCAJ is its collaborative and supportive culture. Team members consistently shared their knowledge, offered guidance, and encouraged one another regardless of role or experience level. During periods of high workload, everyone worked together toward common goals, demonstrating strong teamwork and mutual respect. As an intern, I never felt excluded; instead, I felt genuinely welcomed as part of the team. This positive working culture motivated me to actively participate, contribute, and continuously improve my skills.

### 6. Internship Policies and Support

The internship program provides a flexible and supportive environment that allows interns to balance learning with project responsibilities. Beyond daily assignments, participants are encouraged to attend technical workshops, seminars, and AWS knowledge-sharing sessions. These activities greatly expanded my understanding of cloud computing while exposing me to current industry practices and real-world experiences shared by experienced professionals.

---

## Additional Questions

### What was the most satisfying aspect of your internship?

The aspect I appreciated most was the mentoring style adopted throughout the program. Rather than simply providing answers, mentors encouraged me to investigate problems independently, evaluate different approaches, and propose my own solutions before receiving feedback. This learning method significantly improved my analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and confidence in tackling technical challenges.

### What improvements would you recommend for future interns?

I believe the program could be further improved by organizing additional networking events, technical workshops, or team-building activities. These events would provide interns with more opportunities to interact, exchange experiences, and strengthen collaboration within the community. In addition, incorporating more hands-on laboratories and small real-world projects would allow interns to apply their knowledge more effectively.

### Would you recommend this internship program to your friends? Why?

**Absolutely.** I would confidently recommend First Cloud AI Journey to students interested in Software Engineering, Cloud Computing, and AWS. The program offers an excellent balance between theoretical learning and practical experience. Participants not only gain valuable technical knowledge but also develop professional skills, collaborate within a supportive environment, and gain exposure to industry-standard cloud technologies. It is an outstanding opportunity for anyone seeking to prepare for a future career in technology.

---

## Suggestions and Future Expectations

### Do you have any suggestions for improving the internship experience?

I would recommend expanding the number of collaborative activities, technical workshops, and team-building events to strengthen interaction among interns and mentors. Additionally, introducing more hands-on labs and practical mini-projects based on real business scenarios would help participants gain deeper technical experience and better understand how cloud technologies are applied in professional software development.

### Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?

**Yes.** I would be delighted to continue participating in future FCAJ activities. The program provides an inspiring learning environment where participants are encouraged to explore new technologies, continuously improve their skills, and collaborate with experienced professionals. My internship experience has strengthened both my technical knowledge and professional mindset, motivating me to continue developing my expertise in Software Engineering and Cloud Computing.

### Additional Comments

Finally, I would like to express my sincere appreciation to the entire **First Cloud AI Journey** team, especially the mentors and organizers, for their continuous guidance, encouragement, and willingness to share their knowledge throughout the internship. Their professionalism, enthusiasm, and supportive attitude created a welcoming learning environment that allowed me to grow both technically and personally. This internship has been an invaluable experience that has strengthened my confidence, expanded my professional perspective, and inspired me to continue pursuing a career in Software Engineering while further developing my expertise in cloud technologies.
